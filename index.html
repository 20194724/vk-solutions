Window Cleaning

Interior & exterior window cleaning

Clean windows are one of the fastest ways to make a property look well cared for. We clean glass, frames, sills, and tracks on both the interior and exterior, removing dust, grime, hard-water spots, and streaks so light comes through clearly.

This service is available for high-rise commercial buildings, storefronts, offices, and residential properties, using squeegee and pure-water pole systems suited to the height and type of window.

  • Interior and exterior glass cleaning
  • Frame, sill, and track wipe-down
  • Streak-free, spot-free finish
  • Low-rise and high-rise buildings

Power Washing

Power washing & soft washing

Power washing uses high-pressure water to blast away dirt, mildew, algae, gum, and built-up grime from tough exterior surfaces like sidewalks, parking lots, brick, concrete, and siding — restoring them to a like-new look.

For more delicate surfaces — stucco, painted wood, roofing, or older siding — we use soft washing instead: a lower-pressure application combined with cleaning solutions that lift dirt and kill mold and algae without damaging the surface.

  • High-pressure cleaning for concrete, brick & pavement
  • Low-pressure soft washing for siding & delicate surfaces
  • Removes mildew, algae, gum & built-up grime
  • Great for sidewalks, entryways & building facades
Awning & Storefront Cleaning

Awning & storefront cleaning

Awnings and storefronts are the first thing customers see — and over time they collect dirt, pollen, mildew stains, and faded patches that make a business look neglected. We clean fabric and metal awnings, storefront glass, signage, and entryways so the front of your property makes the right first impression.

We use gentle, surface-appropriate cleaning methods that lift stains and buildup without damaging fabric, seams, or printed branding.

  • Fabric & metal awning cleaning
  • Storefront glass & signage cleaning
  • Mildew & stain removal
  • Restores curb appeal & first impressions
